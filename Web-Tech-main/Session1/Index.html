Welcome to SMI University

Sindh Madrassatul Islam University (SMIU) is a leading institution dedicated to academic excellence, research, and innovation. Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, skills, and character to make a positive impact in society.

Established in 1885, SMIU offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in fields such as Computer Science, Business Administration and Media Sciences.

Our Academic Programs

At SMI University, we offer a variety of programs designed to prepare students for real-world challenges.

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS)
  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
  • Bachelor of Electrical Engineering (BEE)
  • Master of Data Science (MDS)
  • Master of Education (M.Ed.)

Campus Life

Life at SMIU is more than just academics. Our vibrant campus offers a variety of clubs, events, and facilities to help students explore their passions and talents.

  • Modern Library and Digital Learning Center
  • Sports Complex and Gymnasium
  • Student Societies and Cultural Festivals
  • Research and Innovation Labs
